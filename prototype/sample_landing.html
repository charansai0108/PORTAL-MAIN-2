Got Questions?

Is this platform really multi-tenant? ↓
Absolutely! Every institution gets a unique slug (e.g., college-name.portal.com) with its own branding, student database, and specific recruitment cycles.
How do you verify student skills? ↓
We use a combination of AI assessments, manual audits by industry experts, and project-based validation to ensure a "70%+ Industry Ready" score.
How can I get my company listed? ↓
Simply click "Join Now" in the header, choose the Recruiter role, and our team will verify your organization within 24 hours.
