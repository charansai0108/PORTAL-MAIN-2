What's Even This?

A unified, two-sided platform architected for modern colleges and elite companies seeking top-tier talent without the administrative friction.

For Colleges

Manage the entire placement process from one platform.

  • Create and manage placement drives and job opportunities
  • Track student applications and interview progress easily
  • Manage students, recruiters, and placement staff accounts
  • Publish resources, placement events, and announcements
  • Access placement analytics, reports, and activity insights

Run a fully organized placement system without spreadsheets.

For Students

Manage your placement journey in one dashboard.

  • Build a professional profile and upload resumes
  • Explore jobs targeted to your college community
  • Apply to opportunities and track application status
  • Schedule interviews and manage placement events
  • Access placement resources and career guidance

Everything related to placements in one place.

For Companies

Hire directly from verified college talent pools.

  • Post and manage job opportunities across partner colleges
  • Screen qualified candidates and manage incoming applications
  • Run structured interview rounds with organized evaluation workflows
  • Track hiring pipelines, candidate progress, and recruitment analytics
  • Access a streamlined workflow designed for campus recruitment

Find, evaluate, and hire the right candidates through one platform.
